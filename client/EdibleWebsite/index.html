A LITTLE ABOUT OUR PROJECT

Our Initiative

The restaurant industry desperately needs to address the issue of food allergies and other dietary restrictions, because it is becoming increasingly common in todayâ€™s world. In the United States over 15 million people suffer from food allergies, and an additional 31 million are either vegetarian, vegan, or gluten-free. However, the restaurant industry's current practices for accommodating these customers are out of date, slow, and often inaccurate.

Our proposed application would aid those with dietary restrictions by letting them know what they can have at a given restaurant. This would significantly reduce the time it takes for such a person to figure out what they can have at a restaurant. The application is also more accurate and consistent than current methods because the application will have access to the ingredient lists of food items at the specified restaurant.

Our Customers

Any restaurant that is willing to accomodate cutomers dining with food allergies is our target market. The value they would get out of this application is being able to do just that - they would be able to enter their menu information in the application so customers can view it and determine what they can eat.

Our Users

Our target users include any person that is interested in being able to know what they can eat at a restaurant and be aware of any allergens in menu items. Being able to access this information would be incredibly valuable to the entire community living with dietary restrictions today.
